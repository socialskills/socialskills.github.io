Like so many good things in life, the best time to have a connection is before you need it. Whether you're looking for advice, opportunities or a new job, the best time to build those connections is now.

Building connections doesn't come naturally to many introverts. It can be scary! Although it seems hard at first, you can learn the skills needed to build and grow your professional network. The Social Skills for Introverts podcast will teach you those skills through conversations with people that have been there and done that.

As introverts, we can only handle so much social interaction before we need to recharge. This podcast is dedicated to helping you make the most of those social interactions.
